Fund Leadership

Brief bio and background of our fund leadership.

Ochoco Capital Management, LLC was founded and is managed by Steve Weinstein.  Mr. Weinstein has over 25 years of investing experience, primarily covering global high-growth technology and consumer businesses.  He held several leadership positions at M Science, a leading data-driven research firm.  Prior to M Science, he was a partner at Pacific Crest Securities, a leading technology focused investment bank.  Mr. Weinstein has won multiple industry awards including recognition as an Institutional Investor Allstar and Wall Street Journal Best on the Street.
